What the audit actually is

Not a sales call dressed up as a review

The Free Book Audit is a real, structured diagnostic of your QuickBooks Online file. I go through 8 specific areas — the same checklist used on every paid cleanup project — and document what I find with your actual account names, not generic descriptions.

You receive a written findings report within 24 hours. Every issue is listed with its severity (Critical, High, Medium, or Low) and the dollar impact. How much revenue is being understated. How much tax is sitting in the wrong account. How many months are unreconciled.

I do not touch anything in your file. The audit is read-only throughout. The findings are yours whether you work with EcomKeeping afterward or not. There is no pitch during the review. Only documentation.

What gets reviewed

8 specific areas, worked through in order, every time

This is the same structured checklist used on every client file. Nothing is skipped. Nothing is assumed.

01

Revenue Recognition

Are Amazon and Shopify deposits recorded as net deposits or gross sales? This single error affects every number on your P&L.

Critical area
02

Chart of Accounts

Is your QBO built for e-commerce? Separate Amazon and Shopify accounts, clearing accounts, fee categories — or is it a generic template?

Critical area
03

A2X Configuration

Is A2X connected and mapping to the correct accounts? Are clearing accounts zeroing out each settlement period?

Critical area
04

COGS and Inventory

Is cost of goods being tracked at all? Is inventory on the Balance Sheet? What is your real gross margin, not what the P&L currently shows?

Critical area
05

Reconciliation Status

When were accounts last reconciled? How many months are behind? Are clearing accounts showing zero or carrying unexplained balances?

High priority
06

Sales Tax Recording

Is sales tax in an income account or correctly classified as a liability? Which states are you exposed in, and is it being tracked?

Critical area
07

Accounting Method

Cash or accrual? For an inventory-based business, the wrong method creates real problems at tax time and with your CPA.

High priority
08

Books Currency

How current are the books? Transaction gaps, unresolved items, Ask My Accountant balance — what is the real state of the file?

High priority
