What this service is

Not data entry. A complete monthly financial close.

Monthly bookkeeping for an Amazon and Shopify seller is more complex than most bookkeepers realise. You are dealing with two separate payout structures, two different fee architectures, a COGS layer that moves with every sale, and sales tax obligations that vary by state and platform.

EcomKeeping handles all of it. A2X pulls the full settlement data from Amazon and Shopify into QuickBooks Online with every fee category, refund, and reserve correctly mapped. You never see a net deposit recorded as revenue.

Every month closes with a P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statement that are actually accurate. A KPI dashboard showing your real gross margin, ROAS, and AOV. And a plain-English Loom video walking through what happened and what needs your attention.

Delivered by the 5th. Every month. Or the next business day if the 5th falls on a weekend or public holiday.

What every monthly close produces

  • Gross revenue correctly split across Amazon and Shopify
  • Every Amazon fee category separated and recorded
  • COGS tracked and inventory asset updated on the Balance Sheet
  • All bank accounts and clearing accounts reconciled to zero
  • Sales tax correctly classified as a liability, not income
  • Amazon reserve balance reconciled to your Seller Central account
  • P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow exported and delivered
  • KPI dashboard updated with real monthly figures
  • Loom video walkthrough of your numbers
  • Books coordinated with your CPA on request

Scope boundaries

What is not included in this service

EcomKeeping is a specialist bookkeeping service. The following are outside scope and require a different professional.

  • Tax preparation and filing (your CPA or enrolled agent)
  • Sales tax return filing (TaxJar, Avalara, or a sales tax CPA)
  • Payroll processing (Gusto or ADP)
  • CFO services, forecasting, or financial modeling
  • General virtual assistant or admin tasks
  • PPC management, listing optimization, or product research
  • Legal or tax advice of any kind

A note on sales tax

Sales tax monitoring is included in every plan. That means tracking which states you have economic nexus in, flagging when you have crossed a threshold, and making sure sales tax collected is recorded correctly as a liability and not as income.


Filing and remitting sales tax is a separate function that requires a licensed specialist. EcomKeeping does not file sales tax returns. What I hand you and your CPA is a clean Sales Tax Payable balance with a clear breakdown by state.
