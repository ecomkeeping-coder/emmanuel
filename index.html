What we fix

The bookkeeping problems that come standard with selling on Amazon

⚠
Net deposits recorded as revenue

Amazon pays you after deducting fees. If your books record the deposit as revenue, your P&L understates income and misses thousands in deductible expenses every month.

⚠
No COGS tracking

Without a proper COGS setup, your gross margin is meaningless. You cannot price products correctly or know which SKUs are actually making you money.

⚠
Sales tax recorded as income

Tax collected on behalf of a state is a liability, not revenue. When it ends up in your income account, it overstates sales and understates what you owe.

⚠
Books months behind — every year

Handing a CPA 12 months of unreconciled transactions at tax time is expensive. It also means every report you've been looking at was wrong the whole year.

These errors don't just affect your tax return. They affect every business decision you're making right now.

If your gross margin on screen is 80% but your real margin is 42%, everything you're deciding on pricing, ad spend, and inventory is based on numbers that aren't true. A free review of your books shows you exactly where the gap is.

How I work

What working with EcomKeeping actually looks like

Not a black box. Here's exactly what happens month to month, what tools I use, and what you can expect from the relationship.

📑

A2X does the heavy lifting on settlements

Instead of manually categorizing Amazon and Shopify deposits, I use A2X to pull the full settlement data — gross sales, every fee category, every refund — and post it as a structured journal entry in QuickBooks. This is what makes the P&L accurate. No guessing, no net deposit shortcuts.

📊

QuickBooks Online is the single source of truth

Everything lives in your QBO — the chart of accounts, the reconciled transactions, the COGS entries, the tax liabilities. I work inside your file as an accountant user, which means you retain full ownership and access at all times. If we ever part ways, nothing moves with me.

🎥

Every month you get a Loom walkthrough

Once the books close, I record a short Loom video walking through your P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow. I flag anything worth your attention — a margin shift, a fee increase, a clearing account that needs watching. You watch it in 5 minutes instead of decoding a spreadsheet.

💬

Communication is direct and responsive

I'm available 8am–4pm ET Monday through Friday by email. Growth and Scale plan clients also have WhatsApp access for faster back-and-forth. If something urgent comes up outside those hours, I flag it with a Loom note so you see it first thing.

Monthly rhythm

What happens every single month, in order

On the 1st, I start working through the prior month. Amazon settlements matched. Shopify payouts confirmed. Bank and card reconciled. Every transaction accounted for.

5

Monthly call (Growth) or bi-weekly call (Scale) to review the financials together, answer questions, and make sure everything reflects what's actually happening in your business.
