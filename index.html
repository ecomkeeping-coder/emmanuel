Who this is for

If you sell on Amazon or Shopify, there's a good chance your books have hidden errors.

These are the four most common errors in Amazon and Shopify seller files. Each one silently distorts your financials every month.

Net deposits recorded as revenue

Amazon pays after deducting fees. Recording the deposit as revenue understates gross income and misses thousands in deductible expenses.

No COGS tracking

Without COGS, your gross margin is meaningless. You cannot price products correctly or know which SKUs are actually profitable.

Sales tax recorded as income

Tax collected on behalf of a state is a liability. When it sits in an income account it overstates revenue and hides what you owe.

Books months behind every year

Every unreconciled month means more errors compounding. Your CPA charges more. Every report you have been looking at is wrong.
