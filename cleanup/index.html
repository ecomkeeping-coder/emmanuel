Why the books are wrong

The same errors show up in almost every e-commerce file

⚠
Net deposits recorded as revenue

Amazon pays after deducting fees. Recording the deposit as revenue understates gross income, misses thousands in deductible expenses, and makes your P&L meaningless.

⚠
No COGS means no real margin

If product purchases are expensed directly or not recorded at all, your gross margin is completely wrong. You cannot price products correctly or know what you are actually making.

⚠
Sales tax sitting in income

Tax collected on behalf of a state is a liability, not your revenue. When it ends up in an income account, it overstates sales and hides what you owe.

⚠
Months of unreconciled transactions

Every month behind means more errors compounding. Your CPA charges more. Your tax position is unclear. Every report you have been looking at is wrong.
